Privacy policy

Last updated April 12, 2026

This privacy policy describes how TopCallTrackingSoftware ("we," "us," or "the site") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit this website.

Information we collect

We collect minimal information from visitors. Specifically:

  • Server logs. Standard web server access logs including IP address, user agent, referrer, and pages requested. Retained for up to 30 days for security and operational purposes.
  • Analytics. If analytics are enabled on this site, we use a privacy-respecting analytics provider (Plausible or similar) that does not set cross-site cookies and does not collect personal data. Aggregate page views and referrer data only.
  • Email correspondence. If you email us, we retain your email and message for as long as necessary to respond and follow up. We do not add you to any marketing list without explicit opt-in.

What we do not collect

We do not run third-party advertising trackers on this site. We do not use Facebook Pixel, Google Ads remarketing tags, or similar identifiers. We do not sell visitor data.

Cookies

This site does not set marketing or tracking cookies by default. Any cookie used is strictly necessary for site function.

Outbound links and affiliate links

This site contains affiliate links to vendors we review. When you click an affiliate link and sign up for the linked service, the vendor may share information with us about the referral (typically a referral ID and a sign-up confirmation). We don't receive your personal account data from the vendor.

Your rights

If you're in the EU, UK, or California, you have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. Email editors@topcalltrackingsoftware.com to make a request. We respond within 30 days.

Children

This site is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be flagged at the top of this page for at least 30 days.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email editors@topcalltrackingsoftware.com.
